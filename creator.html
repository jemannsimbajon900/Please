The Creators

Batman was created in 1939 by Bob Kane and Bill Finger.

Over the decades, many legendary writers and artists shaped the Batman mythos, including Frank Miller, Scott Snyder, Greg Capullo, Jeph Loeb, and many more.

In the late 1930s, Kane was hired by Detective Comics (which would eventually be known as DC Comics) and was tasked with creating a new superhero to compete with the success of Superman, who had debuted in Action Comics #1 in 1938. Kane came up with the initial design and concept for Batman.

DC Comics

When Batman was created in 1939, Bob Kane and Bill Finger were working for Detective Comics, Inc., which would later be known as DC Comics. Batman first appeared in Detective Comics #27 in May 1939.
